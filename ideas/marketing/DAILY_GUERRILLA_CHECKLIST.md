## üéØ QUICK WIN TACTICS (Pick 3-5, Do Daily)

### Tier 1: Highest Impact, Lowest Effort
1. **Reply to Competitor Conversations** (5 min)
   - Find people complaining about competitors
   - Help them, mention Clodo naturally
   - Expected: 1-2 signups per day

2. **Comment on HN/Reddit** (5 min)
   - Find relevant posts
   - Leave valuable first comment
   - Expected: 50-200 profile views per comment

3. **Engage on Twitter** (10 min)
   - Reply to everyone
   - Find conversations to join
   - Expected: 10-20 profile visits per day

### Tier 2: High Impact, Medium Effort
4. **Create Cheat Sheet/Guide** (2-3 hours, one-time)
   - Post everywhere
   - Email capture
   - Expected: 50-200 emails per guide

5. **Write Migration Guide** (4-6 hours, one-time)
   - High-intent traffic

   - Expected: 200-500 visitors per guide

6. **Post Twitter Thread** (1-2 hours, weekly)
   - High engagement
   - Shareable
   - Expected: 1,000-5,000 impressions per thread

### Tier 3: Strategic, Higher Effort
7. **Guest Post** (3-4 hours per post)
   - Backlinks + traffic
   - Authority building
   - Expected: 100-500 visitors per post

8. **Free Migration Offer** (2-3 hours per migration)
   - Case study + customer
   - High value
   - Expected: 1 case study, 1 potential customer

## üí° PRO TIPS FOR DAILY EXECUTION

1. **Batch Similar Tasks**
   - Do all Twitter replies at once
   - Write all content on one day
   - Schedule distribution

2. **Use Templates**
   - Reply templates for common questions
   - Tweet templates for different content types
   - Email templates for pitches

3. **Set Time Limits**
   - 15 min morning routine (strict!)
   - 15 min evening routine (strict!)
   - Don't get sucked into endless scrolling

4. **Track What Works**
   - Keep simple spreadsheet
   - Note what converts
   - Double down on winners

5. **Stay Consistent**
   - Better to do 15 min daily than 2 hours once a week
   - Momentum > perfection
   - Small wins compound
