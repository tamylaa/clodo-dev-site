Company Overview

Industry: Financial Technology (Regional Bank with $50B+ assets)

Challenge: Modernize legacy payment processing systems handling 10M+ transactions daily while maintaining PCI DSS compliance and zero downtime.

Previous Approach: On-premises infrastructure with custom-built payment gateways, resulting in high maintenance costs and scaling limitations.

The Challenge

The bank needed to modernize their payment infrastructure to handle:

  • 10M+ daily transactions with sub-second processing
  • PCI DSS Level 1 compliance requirements
  • Zero-downtime deployments and maintenance
  • Real-time fraud detection and prevention
  • Seamless integration with existing banking systems
  • Cost-effective scaling during peak holiday periods

Legacy System Issues: High operational costs ($500K/month), frequent outages, manual security updates, and inability to scale during peak loads.

Business Impact: Customer dissatisfaction, compliance risks, and increasing competitive pressure from fintech startups.

The Solution: Zero-Downtime Migration to Clodo Framework

Strategic Decision

After evaluating multiple cloud platforms, the bank chose Clodo Framework for its:

  • Built-in PCI DSS compliance frameworks
  • Zero cold start architecture ensuring instant scaling
  • Enterprise-grade security with end-to-end encryption
  • Proven track record with financial institutions
  • Cost-effective edge computing model

Migration Strategy

Implemented a phased migration approach:

  1. Phase 1 (2 weeks): Parallel deployment and testing
  2. Phase 2 (1 week): Gradual traffic shifting (10% increments)
  3. Phase 3 (1 day): Full cutover with rollback capability
  4. Phase 4 (2 weeks): Legacy system decommissioning

Total Migration Time: 6 weeks with zero downtime and zero transaction losses.
