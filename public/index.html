Why Cloudflare Edge?

Built specifically for the world's most advanced edge network

Global Edge Network

Deploy to 285+ data centers worldwide. Your users get sub-50ms latency regardless of location. No more regional deployments or complex CDN configurations.

Zero Cold Starts

Cloudflare Workers never cold start. Your services are always warm, always fast. Perfect for APIs, webhooks, and real-time applications.

Enterprise Security

Leverage Cloudflare's battle-tested security: DDoS protection, WAF, bot management, and zero-trust networking. Your apps are protected from day one.

Developer Experience

Use familiar JavaScript/TypeScript with modern tooling. No servers to manage, no containers to orchestrate. Focus on code, not infrastructure.
