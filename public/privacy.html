Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Overview

We take privacy seriously. We aim to collect the minimum data necessary to operate our website and communicate with you when you ask us to. We do not sell your data and we do not use invasive tracking.

What we collect

  • Newsletter signups: If you subscribe, we collect your email address and your consent. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails or by contacting us.
  • Support/contact: If you email us, we receive the information you provide (e.g., your email address and message contents).
  • Basic server logs: Our hosting may log standard request information (IP address, user agent, referrer, timestamps) for security and reliability. These logs are retained briefly and used for troubleshooting and abuse prevention.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. We currently do not use any analytics on this website. If analytics is enabled in the future, we will use a privacy-first, cookie-less solution and update this policy accordingly.

How we use your data

  • To send optional product updates and announcements if you opted in.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • To maintain the security and reliability of our services.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited data may be processed by vendors that help us operate the site (for example, hosting and email delivery). These providers are chosen for strong privacy and security practices.

Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email hello@clodo.dev.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and note material changes.
